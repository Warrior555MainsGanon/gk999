IT'S TIME TO REBOOT.


GAMEKNIGHT999 REBOOTED (temporary project codename) is the first and most ambitious fangame project in the history of the Gameknight999 community. As a reboot of the series, we aim to offer our own take on the original trilogy that began the greatest Minecraft tale ever told, featuring:

  • A unique, rebooted take on the trilogy that started the legend.
  • Brand new artstyle and aesthetic to rediscover the world of Minecraft with.
  • Captivating reconstitutions of the books' greatest battles through the brand-new turn-based tactical combat system.
